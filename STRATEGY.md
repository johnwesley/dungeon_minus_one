# Dungeon-1 Strategy Guide

This guide outlines the optimal path to collect all 13 treasures and complete the game.

## Overview
- **Goal**: Collect 13 treasures and deposit them in the **Living Room** trophy case.
- **Victory Condition**: All treasures in Living Room -> Secret door opens -> Enter Vault.

## Required Treasures
1.  **Jeweled Egg** (`jeweled_egg`) - Up a Tree
2.  **Silver Chalice** (`chalice`) - Treasure Room
3.  **Bag of Coins** (`bag_of_coins`) - Maze (Skeleton Alcove)
4.  **Jade Figurine** (`jade_figurine`) - Bat Room
5.  **Sapphire Bracelet** (`sapphire_bracelet`) - Gas Room
6.  **Platinum Bar** (`platinum_bar`) - Loud Room
7.  **Trunk of Jewels** (`trunk_of_jewels`) - Reservoir
8.  **Crystal Trident** (`crystal_trident`) - Atlantis Room
9.  **Ivory Torch** (`ivory_torch`) - Torch Room
10. **Gold Coffin** (`gold_coffin`) - Egyptian Room
11. **Pot of Gold** (`pot_of_gold`) - End of Rainbow
12. **Scarab** (`scarab`) - Sandy Cave
13. **Crystal Skull** (`crystal_skull`) - Land of the Dead

## Essential Tools
- **Lantern**: Living Room
- **Sword**: Living Room (needed for Troll/Cyclops)
- **Rope**: Attic (needed for Torch Room / slide)
- **Nasty Knife**: Attic
- **Garlic**: Kitchen (repels Bat)
- **Wrench**: Maintenance Room (Dam)
- **Screwdriver**: Maintenance Room (Dam)
- **Air Pump**: Reservoir North
- **Plastic Boat**: Dam Base (implied/needed for river)
- **Shovel**: Sandy Beach (needed for Scarab)
- **Skeleton Key**: Maze (Skeleton Alcove)

## Walkthrough

### Phase 1: Preparation & Surface
1.  **Start**: Go `north`, `east` to **Behind House**. Open window (`open window`), enter `kitchen`.
2.  **Kitchen**: Take `garlic`, `bottle`, `brown_sack`. Go `up` to **Attic**.
3.  **Attic**: Take `rope`, `nasty_knife`. Go `down` to Kitchen, then `west` to **Living Room**.
4.  **Living Room**: Take `brass_lantern`, `elvish_sword`.
5.  **Treasure #1 (Chalice)**:
    -   From Living Room, go `west` (Strange Passage) -> `west` (Cyclops Room).
    -   Go `up` to **Treasure Room**. Take `chalice`.
    -   Return to Living Room.
6.  **Treasure #2 (Jeweled Egg)**:
    -   From Living Room, `east` -> `out` (Behind House) -> `north` (North of House) -> `north` (Forest Path).
    -   Go `up` to **Up a Tree**. Take `jeweled_egg`.
    -   Descend.

### Phase 2: The Maze & Key
1.  **Enter Maze**:
    -   From House, go `down` into **Cellar**.
    -   Go `north` to **Troll Room**. Kill/Fight Troll with Sword.
    -   Go `west` to **Maze Entrance**.
2.  **Treasure #3 (Bag of Coins)**:
    -   Navigate to **Maze (Skeleton Alcove)** (`west` from entrance -> `south` -> `up`? *Note: Maze navigation may vary; follow standard Zork paths or map: Entrance -> West -> Dead End? No.*
    -   *Logic*: Find `maze_skeleton`. Take `bag_of_coins` and `skeleton_key`.
3.  **Exit Maze**:
    -   Return to **Living Room** to deposit treasures found so far (`chalice`, `jeweled_egg`, `bag_of_coins`).

### Phase 3: The Deep (Coal Mine & Gas)
1.  **Enter Mine**:
    -   From Living Room -> `down` -> `north` -> `west` (Maze) -> Navigate to **Shaft Room** or **Gas Room** via slide/ladder?
    -   Alternative: **Dome Room** / **Torch Room** path.
    -   Let's go via **Cellar** -> `north` -> `east` -> **East-West Passage** -> `east` -> **Round Room**.
    -   `Round Room` -> `east` -> **Loud Room**.
2.  **Treasure #4 (Platinum Bar)**:
    -   In **Loud Room**, it is loud. Usually need to stop noise (Dam) or just grab it? Echo causes issues. Grab `platinum_bar`.
3.  **Treasure #5 (Jade Figurine)**:
    -   Go to **Bat Room** (via Shaft Room). Have `garlic` to pass Bat. Take `jade_figurine`.
4.  **Treasure #6 (Sapphire Bracelet)**:
    -   Go to **Gas Room**. Take `sapphire_bracelet`.
5.  **Treasure #7 (Coal Mine)**:
    -   Navigate deeper to **Coal Mine**. (No treasure here directly, but path to Ladder).

### Phase 4: The River & Dam
1.  **Dam Control**:
    -   Go to **Dam Lobby** -> **Maintenance Room**. Take `wrench`, `screwdriver`.
    -   Go to **Dam**. Activate controls (`bolt` + `button` + `wrench`) to close sluice gates? Or open? Goal: Drain reservoir or fill it?
    -   *Strategy*: Close gates to drain reservoir to reach `trunk_of_jewels`.
2.  **Treasure #8 (Trunk of Jewels)**:
    -   Go `north` to **Reservoir South** -> `north` to **Reservoir**.
    -   If drained, take `trunk_of_jewels`.
3.  **Treasure #9 (Crystal Trident)**:
    -   From Reservoir, go `north` to **Reservoir North** -> `north` to **Atlantis Room**.
    -   Take `crystal_trident`.
4.  **Treasure #10 (Pot of Gold)**:
    -   Go to **Dam Base**. Inflate `plastic_boat` with `air_pump`.
    -   Launch boat. Go downstream to **White Cliffs** / **Aragain Falls**.
    -   Land at **Sandy Beach**? Or **End of Rainbow**.
    -   Take `pot_of_gold` at **End of Rainbow**.
    -   *Note*: Ensure you have the `shovel`.
5.  **Treasure #11 (Scarab)**:
    -   At **Sandy Beach**, use `shovel` to dig in **Sandy Cave** (northeast). Take `scarab`.

### Phase 5: The Temple & Hades
1.  **Treasure #12 (Ivory Torch)**:
    -   Go to **Torch Room** (via Dome Room? or Temple?).
    -   Take `ivory_torch`.
2.  **Treasure #13 (Gold Coffin)**:
    -   From Torch Room -> **Temple** -> **Egyptian Room**.
    -   Take `gold_coffin`.
3.  **Treasure #14 (Crystal Skull)**:
    -   Go to **Altar** -> `down` -> **Cave** -> `down` -> **Entrance to Hades**.
    -   Pass spirits (Ring Bell? Candle? Book? - Ritual needed if spirits block).
    -   Enter **Land of the Dead**. Take `crystal_skull`.

### Phase 6: Victory
1.  Return to **Living Room**.
2.  Ensure all treasures are in the room (drop them).
3.  Trophy Case opens -> Secret Door.
4.  Enter Secret Door to **Victory**.
