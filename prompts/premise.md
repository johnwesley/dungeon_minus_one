## Game Premise
The player is dropped into a quiet clearing beside a mysterious white house, with no explanation of how they got there or why the place looks like it’s been abandoned since before electricity was invented. Exploration leads the player inside, where they eventually stumble upon a hidden entrance to the shattered remains of the **Great Underground Empire**.

Centuries earlier, this empire sprawled beneath the land like some unhinged architectural fever dream. Its ruler, **Lord Dimwit Flathead the Excessive**, spent his reign building absurd monuments, magical infrastructure, and a labyrinth of chambers that made sense only to whatever committee designed them. Naturally, the whole civilization collapsed under the weight of its own ridiculousness, leaving behind forgotten machines, dangerous creatures, and puzzles that feel like the Empire’s final attempt at petty revenge.

The player steps into these ruins as an accidental adventurer turned treasure hunter. Their task is to navigate the ruins of the fallen Empire, collect its lost treasures, deposit them in the trophy case above, and earn enough progress to unlock passage into the deeper, weirder parts of the underground world.
